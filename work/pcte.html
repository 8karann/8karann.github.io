02 — The System

Trend analysis as a weekly ritual

The growth engine was a repeatable loop, not lucky virality:

Fig. 01 — Weekly organic growth loopOperating rhythm

Scan

Mon–Tue
Audit trending audios, formats and campus-relevant memes; shortlist what fits the college's voice and can be shot within the week.

Produce

Wed–Thu
Brief student teams, shoot fast, edit for the first 2 seconds — hook, caption, audio timing all tuned to retention.

Learn

Fri–Sun
Publish at peak windows, track reach/saves/shares, fold what worked into next week's calendar.
