As Marketing Executive at a Dubai solar energy company, I owned the full loop — strategy, production, paid media and analytics. Five months in: 120K+ cumulative video views, ~35% engagement lift, and steadily cheaper clicks on an AED 4,000/month budget.

Solar is a considered, high-ticket B2B/B2C hybrid purchase in a sceptical market. The job wasn't to go viral — it was to make a technical brand consistently visible, credible and cheaper to reach month over month.

02 — Content System

Short-form as infrastructure

I produced and edited short-form video for Instagram, LinkedIn and YouTube on a fixed cadence — product explainers, install showcases and offer-led creatives — coordinating an intern team and a graphic designer to keep the pipeline publishing on schedule.

  • Brand guidelines from scratch — I autonomously developed the visual and content guidelines that kept every asset, from reels to product posters, recognisably Euronet.
  • Platform-native cuts — the same shoot became a 20s IG reel, a LinkedIn credibility post and a YouTube explainer, each edited to its platform's retention pattern.
  • AI-accelerated workflow — scripting, hooks and storyboards drafted with AI tools, then produced in Premiere Pro / DaVinci — keeping a one-person editing seat at agency output.

03 — Testing & Media

Structured A/B testing, not guesswork

The AED 4,000/month budget across Meta and Google Ads was treated as a testing lab. Each cycle followed the same loop:

Fig. 04 — Creative testing loopMethodology

Hypothesis

One variable per test — hook, thumbnail, CTA copy, or landing page section

Split run

A/B variants at matched budgets and audiences on Meta / Google

Read

GA4 + Ads Manager: CPC, CTR, engagement trend, lead quality — not vanity reach

Reallocate

Winners scaled, losers cut, learnings folded into the next creative brief

GA4 and Meta performance data fed back into growth strategy adjustments — which content themes earned watch time, which audiences produced quote-ready leads rather than clicks, and where landing pages leaked conversions.

What this role proves about how I work

Give me a modest budget and a lean team, and I'll build the full system — brand, content pipeline, paid media and measurement — and improve it every month with disciplined testing.
