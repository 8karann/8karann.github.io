How I Think

03 — Process
01 / RESEARCH

Audience before ads

Personas, search behaviour, community listening and competitor benchmarking. Every brief starts with who we're actually talking to — SEMrush, GA4 and raw comment sections included.

02 / STRATEGY

Frameworks, not vibes

STP, AIDA funnels, SMART objectives and 7Ps — translated into channel plans, budget splits and content pillars a team can actually execute against.

03 / EXECUTION

Ship fast, ship native

Platform-native short-form video, batch production systems and AI-accelerated workflows. I edit, write and launch — no waiting on a production team.

04 / MEASUREMENT

Test, learn, reallocate

A/B creative tests, CPC/CPA tracking, engagement-rate analysis. Winners get budget, losers get killed, and every campaign feeds the next hypothesis.
